About Us

Welcome to DormPro

Welcome to DormPro, where we revolutionize the university experience for students seeking seamless living and dining solutions. At DormPro, we're committed to simplifying campus life by offering a one-stop platform that empowers university students to effortlessly reserve dormitory rooms with their friends and manage refectory tickets digitally.

Our user-friendly app is designed to foster community connections, allowing you to choose your dorm mates and streamline your dining hall experience. With DormPro, say goodbye to the complexities of room reservations and paper tickets – we're here to make your university journey more convenient, connected, and enjoyable. Welcome to a smarter way of living and dining on campus with DormPro!
